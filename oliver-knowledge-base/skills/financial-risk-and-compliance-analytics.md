## Summary
Domain skill focused on building compliant analytics pipelines and decision systems in regulated environments.
Practical coverage includes AML/KYC, audit readiness, claims-data compliance, and investment risk analysis.

## Capability Areas
- AML/KYC workflow analytics and anomaly detection.
- Audit-readiness controls and data traceability design.
- Compliance-aware integration under regulations such as HIPAA and NY DFS.
- Financial scenario analysis and risk-informed decision support.
